# Lead Qualification Scoring Rubric (v1)

*Date: 2026-04-24 / Version: 1.0*
*Owner: CMO*

## 1. Purpose
This rubric is designed to standardize the evaluation of incoming inquiries for UniteCube's business automation services. It ensures that the sales team prioritizes leads with the highest potential for long-term retention and successful implementation.

---

## 2. Scoring Criteria

| Category | 3 Points (High) | 2 Points (Medium) | 1 Point (Low) | 0 Points (N/A) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Manual Pain Points** | High-volume repetitive tasks (e.g., 50+ inquiries/week or 10+ hours manual data entry). | Moderate volume (20-50 inquiries/week). | Low volume, but bothersome. | No clear repetitive task identified. |
| **Technology Fit** | Already using LINE Official Account, WordPress, and Google Sheets. | Using some cloud tools (Gmail, Excel) but no automation-friendly APIs. | Primarily paper-based or legacy desktop software. | Hostile to cloud-based solutions. |
| **Authority** | Representative Director, Owner, or CEO. | Department Manager or Decision Influencer. | Junior staff or temporary contractor. | No authority to make financial decisions. |
| **Budget Alignment** | Comfortable with 50k+ setup and 25k/mo management fee. | Hesitant on setup fee but sees value in monthly management. | Looking for a "one-time" cheap fix under 10k. | Seeking free advice or "do-it-for-me" for free. |
| **Timeline** | Needs solution within 2 weeks. | Planning to implement within 1-2 months. | Just "browsing" or researching for next year. | No defined timeline. |
| **Geographic Focus** | Based in Wakkanai or Soya Subprefecture (Hokkaido). | Other regions within Hokkaido. | Outside Hokkaido (Rest of Japan). | International (Out of scope). |

---

## 3. Qualification Summary

Calculate the total score (Max 18 points) to determine the next action:

### **A: Hot Lead (14 - 18 Points)**
*   **Profile:** Decision-maker in Hokkaido with high manual workload and budget ready.
*   **Action:** Immediate response (within 2 hours). Schedule a 30-minute hearing session. Aim for contract within 7 days.

### **B: Warm Lead (9 - 13 Points)**
*   **Profile:** Interested but might have budget constraints or lower volume.
*   **Action:** Response within 24 hours. Send specific case studies relevant to their industry. Invite to "Automation Demo" or LINE consultation.

### **C: Cold / Nurture (5 - 8 Points)**
*   **Profile:** Small-scale or low-tech readiness.
*   **Action:** Add to monthly newsletter. Provide "Getting Started with Digitalization" PDF. Low-priority follow-up.

### **D: Disqualified (< 5 Points)**
*   **Profile:** No budget, no authority, or completely out of region/scope.
*   **Action:** Polite refusal or referral to general software tools (e.g., "We recommend trying Zapier yourself").
