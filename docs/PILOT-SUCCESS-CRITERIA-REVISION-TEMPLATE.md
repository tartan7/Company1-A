## 1. Purpose
Use this template when pilot success criteria must be revised after kickoff due to changing business priorities, measurement constraints, or new evidence.
Document the rationale, impact, approval decision, and implementation steps before revised criteria are treated as the new baseline.

---

## 2. Revision Trigger

- **Business Priority Shift:** Commercial objective changed (for example: volume to quality).
- **Measurement Integrity Issue:** Existing KPI is not reliably measurable or auditable.
- **Operating Reality Change:** Delivery constraints or dependencies changed expected outcomes.
- **Learning-Driven Adjustment:** New data indicates the original threshold is unrealistic or too conservative.
