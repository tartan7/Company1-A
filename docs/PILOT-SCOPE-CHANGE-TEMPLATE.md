## 1. Purpose
Use this template when pilot scope must be expanded, reduced, or re-prioritized after kickoff.
Document the business rationale, delivery impact, and approval decision before execution.

---

## 2. Change Type

- **Scope Expansion:** Add workflows/channels/features not in signed pilot scope.
- **Scope Reduction:** Remove or defer committed pilot deliverables.
- **Scope Re-prioritization:** Keep same overall scope, but change order or emphasis.
- **Commercial Change:** Any adjustment that affects fees, timeline, or contractual terms.
