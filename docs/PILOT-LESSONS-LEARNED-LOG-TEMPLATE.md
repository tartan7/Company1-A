## 1. Purpose
Use this template to capture pilot lessons as they happen, not only at closeout.
The log converts observed wins/failures into reusable standards, controls, and playbook updates.

---

## 2. When to Log a Lesson

Add a lesson entry when any of the following occurs:

- A repeated issue is resolved with a new method worth standardizing.
- A KPI/result materially overperforms due to a specific intervention.
- A risk, dependency, or assumption behaves differently than expected.
- A preventable failure occurs and root cause is understood.
- A stakeholder process decision changes execution outcomes.

Do not wait for monthly reviews; log within 2 business days of validation.

## 4. Status Definitions

- **New:** Lesson recorded; pending validation or follow-up.
- **Validated:** Lesson is supported by evidence and agreed by owner.
- **Implemented:** Recommendation has been embedded in a governed artifact/process.
- **Archived:** Lesson kept for historical reference; no further action required.

Only mark `Implemented` when the linked artifact update is complete and auditable.

---

## 5. Lesson Quality Checklist

Before setting a lesson to `Validated` or `Implemented`, confirm:

1. The lesson statement is specific and evidence-backed.
2. Impact on `scope/timeline/KPI/risk` is explicitly captured.
3. Recommendation is actionable and reusable.
4. A standardization target is named.
5. One owner and due date are assigned.

---

## 6. Review Cadence

1. Review all `New` lessons weekly in the pilot governance checkpoint.
2. Promote high-confidence lessons to `Validated` with evidence links.
3. Convert top lessons into control, SOP, or template updates within the same pilot phase when feasible.
4. Carry unresolved high-impact lessons into remediation/action trackers.
5. Include top 3 implemented lessons in pilot closeout and success-story artifacts.
