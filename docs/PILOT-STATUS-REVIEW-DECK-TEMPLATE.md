# Pilot Status-Review Deck Template (v1)

*Date: 2026-04-25 / Version: 1.0*
*Owner: CMO*
*Primary Audience: Client Sponsor + UniteCube Leadership*
*Recommended Cadence: Weekly during active pilot, biweekly when stable*

## 1. Purpose
Use this template to build a decision-ready pilot status-review deck.
The deck should communicate performance, delivery health, risks, and decisions in under 15 minutes.

---

## 3. Recommended Slide Order (12 Slides)

1. Cover and meeting objective
2. Executive snapshot (RAG + topline movement)
3. KPI scorecard and trend
4. Outcome highlights and proof points
5. Delivery and milestone health
6. Risk and blocker heatmap
7. Dependencies and decision timeline
8. Budget and resource view (if in scope)
9. Scope changes and decision log highlights
10. Next 14-30 day plan
11. Leadership asks and decision summary
12. Appendix (detail tables, definitions, backup charts)

Target duration by section:
- Slides 1-2: 3 minutes
- Slides 3-8: 8 minutes
- Slides 9-11: 4 minutes
- Slide 12: as needed

## 4. Slide-by-Slide Build Template

## 6. Visual and Formatting Standards

- Keep slide density low: max 35 words per content box where possible.
- Use consistent RAG colors across every slide:
  - Green = on track, no immediate intervention required
  - Yellow = watch condition, action underway
  - Red = off track, leadership intervention required
- Use one chart type per concept:
  - Trend: line chart
  - Comparison: bar chart
  - Risk concentration: heatmap
- Keep date format consistent: `YYYY-MM-DD`
- Put source timestamp on KPI charts (e.g., `Data refresh: YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM UTC`)

---

## 7. Presenter Notes Script (Optional)

Use this simple script structure per slide:

1. `What changed since last review`
2. `Why it matters`
3. `What action is underway`
4. `What decision/support is needed`

This keeps the review focused on outcomes and decisions rather than raw activity.
