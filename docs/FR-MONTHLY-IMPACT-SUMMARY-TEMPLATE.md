## 5. Field Definitions (Fill Guide)

- **Adoption Metrics:** Number of successful real-user/admin uses of the FR in the month. Include `% MoM` when prior-month data exists.
- **Incidents:** Count of FR-related incidents raised in the month, with highest severity observed (`Low / Medium / High`).
- **Support Questions:** Count of support tickets/messages linked to FR usage; add the dominant question theme.
- **Completion Lead Time (days):** Calendar days from FR start (`in_progress`) to production-ready completion (`done`).
- **Stakeholder Impact Summary:** One sentence on business/user impact (time saved, error reduction, confidence gain, or revenue effect).
- **Status (KPI Rollup):** `On Track` when within monthly target or improving trend; otherwise `At Risk`.
