## 1. Purpose
Use this template to deliver a concise, decision-ready executive summary for pilot reporting.
Prioritize business outcomes, major risks, and clear asks over detailed activity logs.

## 12. One-Page Variant (Recommended for Busy Sponsors)

Use this compressed layout when the audience needs a fast read in under two minutes.

Required sections on one page:
- Executive snapshot (status, top outcome, top risk, top decision)
- 3 KPI outcome table (current vs target vs variance)
- Top 2 risks/dependencies with owner + date
- Top 2 executive asks with due dates
- Next 2 priorities for the next 14 days

Compression rules:
- Max 6 bullets total outside tables
- Max 3 KPIs
- Max 2 risks and 2 asks

---

## 13. Two-Page Variant (Recommended for Steering Committees)

Use this layout when leadership needs slightly more context but still expects concise decision support.

Page 1:
- Metadata and executive snapshot
- Strategic outcome summary table
- Highlights and watchouts

Page 2:
- Risk and dependency summary
- Executive decisions required
- Next-period priorities
- Optional financial/resource note

Expansion rules:
- Keep each page independently readable
- Preserve owner + date on every risk and ask
