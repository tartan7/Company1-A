## 1. Purpose
Use this template to convert pilot outcomes into a credible, reusable success story for internal enablement and external marketing.
Complete it within 5 business days after a pilot phase closes.

## 2. Story Readiness Checklist

Mark all items before publishing.

- [ ] KPI evidence is validated against the source dashboard/report.
- [ ] Baseline window and comparison logic are explicit.
- [ ] Client approval for naming, quote, and logo usage is documented.
- [ ] Sensitive values are redacted if approval is limited.
- [ ] Final story owner and publish channels are assigned.
