## 1. Purpose
Use this template for recurring governance checkpoints during pilot delivery.
The checkpoint validates pilot health, decisions, risks, dependencies, and scope discipline before issues become executive escalations.

## 3. Entry Criteria (Pre-Checkpoint Readiness)

Confirm all required artifacts are current before the governance meeting:

1. Decision log updated through the checkpoint date.
2. Risk register reviewed and re-scored within past 7 days.
3. KPI anomaly log checked and open incidents status refreshed.
4. Scope-change requests submitted for any non-trivial changes.
5. Dependency map reviewed for owner, date, and escalation path.

If any item is missing, record as `Governance Debt` in Section 8.

## 6. Exit Criteria (Checkpoint Completion)

A checkpoint is complete only when all items are true:

1. RAG status assigned for all assessment domains.
2. All `Red` items have explicit owner, due date, and escalation path.
3. New decisions are logged in the pilot decision log.
4. Risk register, dependency map, and stakeholder update are synchronized.
5. Next checkpoint date and participant roster are confirmed.

**Checkpoint Outcome:** Passed / Passed with Conditions / Failed

## 9. Suggested Checkpoint Cadence

- Weekly during active implementation or unstable KPI periods.
- Biweekly only when all domains are `Green` for 2 consecutive checkpoints.
- Immediate ad hoc checkpoint when any Sev-1/Sev-2 KPI incident occurs.
