1. Brand Foundation

Brand promise: UniteCube helps SMB teams run operations with confidence through clear, dependable workflow automation.

Attributes: Trustworthy, clear, practical, friendly, reliable.

Positioning: Enterprise-grade reliability without enterprise complexity.
