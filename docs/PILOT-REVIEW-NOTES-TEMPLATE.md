## 1. Purpose
Use this template to capture structured review notes during pilot governance, status, quality, or milestone reviews.
Keep notes factual, evidence-based, and action-oriented so follow-through can be tracked without losing context.

## 9. Closure Check

Mark review notes complete only when:

1. All material observations are logged with owners.
2. Decisions are added to the decision log.
3. High-priority actions have due dates and dependencies.
4. Escalation-required items have named escalation owners.
5. Next review date and agenda focus are defined.
